move round Hold hands together and turn right or left. Related dances are druiekusman, almelosekermis, and de vleegerd.
Small round All arms crossed over chest, turn clockwise. Related dances are Afklappertje and Druiekusman
HOOK ELBOW
AND TURN Hook each other's elbows and turn in place. Related dance is Riepe Riepe Garste.
Hold one hand
and turn Hold each other's one hand and turn with other hand on waist. Related dance is Afklappertje
clap Usually, while a pair dances, others clap in place. Or the various clapping sounds themselves can create dance moves. Related dances are Afklappertje, Riepe Riepe Garste, and druiekusman.
