move round Hold hands together and turn right or left. Related dances are druiekusman, almelosekermis, boanopstekker, Zevensprong and de vleegerd.
Polka polka or slow polka, starting with the man’s left and woman’s right foot turning clockwise with partner, progressing in line of dance. Related dances are Afklappertje, Keppelse Quadrille and Druiekusman
HOOK ELBOW
AND TURN Hook each other's elbows and turn in place. Related dances are Riepe Riepe Garste, Keppelse Quadrille.
Hold one hand
and turn Hold each other's one hand and turn with other hand on waist. One pair can walk slowly or run fast in this position. Related dances are Afklappertje, Keppelse Quadrille
clap Usually, while a pair dances, others clap in place. Or the various clapping sounds themselves can create dance moves. Related dances are Afklappertje, Riepe Riepe Garste, and druiekusman.
Stamp Usually, at the end of a move, a pair faces each other and rolls their feet alternately in place(3 times). Related dances are Almelosekermis, Druiekusman, and Riepe Riepe Garste
forefinger up Shake one forefinger toward the other person. Place the other hand on the waist. Related dance is Druiekusman
Turn without
holding hands With all arms crossed over the chest, or both hands on the waist, make a small clockwise circle. Related dance is Almelose Kermis
Kick Kick in the air at the end of a dance move. Related dance is Slaapmuts
facing each other
in a row Everyone stands facing each other in a row with their hands at the waist. Usually they all move to the left and right or add back and forth together. Related dance are Slaapmuts, Blauw garen en koperdraad
grab hands and turn Hold your partner's hands and rotate. Related dances are Slaapmuts, Blauw garen en koperdraad, and Keppelse Quadrille
Singer Sing alone, lead the group. Related dance is Keppelse Quadrille
balance The couples balance, or rocking side to side starting from the right. Related dance is Keppelse Quadrille
Jump in place(circle) All together hold hands, stand in a circle, and jump in place. Related dances are almelose kermis, boanopstekker, and Zevensprong
Jump in place(row) Standing in line, facing each other, jumping on the spot. They jump together, or jump alternately line by line. Related dance is Blauw garen en koperdraad
Each turn Rotate 360 degrees in place or rotate 180 degrees to come back and do the next dance move. Related dance is boanopstekker
one hand stretching toward one direction Forward or backward, raising the arms of holding hands. Related dance is de larense polka, lot is dood.
Approaching The two pairs facing each other hold each partner's hand and go toward the center and then go into place. In addition to this movement, partners also switch positions with each other. Related dance is de vleegerd
