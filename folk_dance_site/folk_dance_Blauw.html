name

Blauw garen en koperdraad

Description

a newly made-up dance by Marita Kruijswijk to the melody from farm straws and contra dances. "Blauw garen en koperdraad(Blue yarn and copper wire)" is a Dutch folk dance from the 17th century and the melody is recorded between 1700 and 1716 in the bundle: "Oude en Nieuwe Hollantse Boerenlieties en Contredansen". A reprint of this music collection was published in 1974. Taken from 'Hopsa', by Marita Kruijswijk with illustrations by Aafje Dijkhuizen.

Area
