name

Riepe Riepe Garste

Description

Riepe Riepe Garste is a dance choreographed by Mrs. Sanson Cat, but studies by Cees Temple have found two variations in eastern Holland. After the clapping motion, various variations appear in mimicking the movement of cutting corn. Also, after all the moves are over, there is a way for a woman to walk to a new partner on the right, keep dancing and change her partner.
