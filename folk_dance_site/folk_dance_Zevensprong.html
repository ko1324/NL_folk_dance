name

Zevensprong

Description

The dance is performed in many places with many different variants depending on the origin of the dance. Across Europe, the seven jumps are being performed in a variety of ways. After making your head touch the ground, the song is sung again in this position with the movements done in reverse order with one the head up, with two one elbow and so on until everyone is standing again, or omits the process and starts right from the beginning.
